Toronto

I always had a dream back in the Congo...One day, I wanted to live my life in a big, beautiful city, full of colors and lights with tall buildings. Never did I expect that in my life time, I would get to live in this beautiful country and visit big cities that many foreigners are attracted to. It was a huge success for me when I landed in the beautiful city of Toronto. There was something amazing about this ciy that no other city has. It has a unique multi cultural environment where everyone gets together which the Congo lacked, and for the most part, I felt like I landed in the right place to develop my I.T Skills, and to me there was no better choice than Canada.

I am a Programmer

Back in the Congo, i did not know much about codes and programming languages. I did know some things about it, and the logic behind the codes sounded very interesting to me. During our computer lectures back in the school days, it was the only class my ears would be highly active to listen, but sadly during other classes, I felt very sleepy and tired of taking all that information from the lectures. At a point, the only thing that i would hear from teachers were sounds like "bla bla bla..." No offence LOL, but when you have a passion and dream set for yourself, nothing else is understandable. Once i came to Georgian College, I started understanding what programming was all about, and here i am now!
